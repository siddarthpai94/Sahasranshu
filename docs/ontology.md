# Stance Ontology

## Stance Categories

- **Supportive**: Policy explicitly supports a position
- **Cautious**: Policy indicates caution or concerns
- **Opposed**: Policy explicitly opposes a position
- **Neutral**: Policy takes no clear stance
- **Unclear**: Unable to determine stance from text

## Evidence Grounding

Each stance must be grounded in:
- Page references (page number, section)
- Direct quotes or paraphrasing
- Context indicators
