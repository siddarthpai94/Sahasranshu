# Datasets

## Source Documentation

Documents are sourced from official policy repositories:
- Federal Reserve (US)
- Reserve Bank of India (RBI)
- Other central banks

## Provenance Rules

- All documents are tracked via manifest files
- Content hashes ensure integrity
- Source URLs and acquisition dates are recorded
- No raw PDFs are committed to the repository

## Data Organization

- Sample documents go in `data/samples/`
- Manifests go in `data/manifests/`
- Case study examples go in `examples/*/`
