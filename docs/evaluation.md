# Evaluation Metrics

## Key Metrics

### Consistency
Measures internal logical consistency of extracted stances within a single document.

### Stability
Measures consistency of stance classifications across multiple runs.

### Grounding
Measures quality of evidence citations and page references.

## Regression Testing

Golden outputs are stored in `examples/*/expected_outputs/` for:
- Stance extraction validation
- Delta detection accuracy
- Hypothesis quality
